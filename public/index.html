Timeline

  • 1894-  A&M established football team.  Compiled a 1-1 record in first season
  • 1909-  Coach Charley Moran is hired.  A&M's first undefeated season
  • 1914-  Charley Moran leaves after compiling a 38-8-4 record.  Edwin Harlan is hired to replace him
  • 1915-  A&M joins the southwest conference along with Texas,  Baylor,  Arkansas, Rice,   Oklahoma,  and Oklahoma St.  (Oklahoma and Oklahoma St. would leave in 1919 and 1925 respectively)
  • 1917-  TAMU hires Dana X. Bible to be head coach.  Coach Bible led the aggies to an undefeated season in 1917.  Coach Bible would leave A&M for LSU.
  • 1919-  A&M rehired coach Bible.  His 1919 team went undefeated, outscoring opponents 275-0.  The 1919 team was named national champion.
  • 1922-  The 12th man tradtion is started when Tamu only had 18 players in a game against Centre College.  Players were starting to get injured so Coach Bible called on a reserve player in the press box named E. King Gill to suit up for the game,  thus the tradtion was born.
